My Love Story: How It All Began

My life truly started to become beautiful when I was 21 years old. Why, you ask? Because I finally got the girl of my dreams. To be honest, calling her just a "girlfriend" doesn't even do it justice; she had been my biggest crush for the longest time. I was absolutely crazy about her! Once, I confessed my feelings to her, but she just looked at me and asked, "Are you crazy?" After that, I distanced myself a bit and kept a low profile, acting a little mysterious. However, I couldn't hide the love I had for her in my heart for very long. Eventually, I started hanging out and traveling with her and her friends. We went hiking and on little trips—basically, we got into a nice travel routine.

The Turning Point in Polonnaruwa

Somehow or another, we all ended up going on a trip to Polonnaruwa. That was the day all those buried feelings for her came rushing back to the surface. We even went to worship at the Somawathiya Stupa, traveling there in a small tractor. The best part of the whole trip? I somehow managed to find a way to sit right next to her. I stayed by her side the entire way there and back, talking a total ear off and cracking silly jokes like an idiot. I have no idea what she thought of me that day! After that trip, though, we started talking more frequently again.

A Unique Proposal

Things kept moving forward, and on September 1, 2022, I finally asked her to be my girlfriend. And guess what? She said yes! I actually asked her to be my girlfriend in a very unique way. We were having a conversation about buying cars, and I told her, "Let's buy a car together." I added that I wouldn't get a driver's license because I'd just hire a driver instead, and I invited her to join me on the ride. That was how she agreed to get into that car, and ultimately, how she walked right into my life. Since that day, so many things have happened in our lives. Those memories are incredibly beautiful. Our very first trip together as a couple was to Kandy, and it was absolutely wonderful! Well, that is where I will wrap up today's chapter. I'll be back to tell you the rest of the story on the next page. I'll also drop a few of our photos below. There is no love in this world that can ever compare to ours. Until next time, I'm signing off!
